TECHNOLOGY?

Most innovations of this time were not of the physical kind,
most were within the internet itself.
But there is one important innovation that came in 2007,
the Iphone. Which revolutionized the way webbsites needed to be built.

The Iphone required that webbsites be flexible in the way that they were built.
No longer could you define pictures and text in only pixels,
a new method of scaling was needed.
