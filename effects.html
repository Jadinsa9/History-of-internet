CONSEQUENCES
OF THE
IT-BUBBLE

Considering the name "IT-Bubble burst" you would think that something terrible happened.
However the event is the very reason why we have large search engines such as Google,
which survived the burst with its keyword-based advertising.
The burst also left a lot of extra capacity now suddenly available after the market was cleaned of companies.
Leading to very affordable and fast connectivity that led to more people buying it for their own homes.
